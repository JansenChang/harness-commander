# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Harness-Commander to be an open, respectful, and constructive project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that help build a positive environment include:

- being respectful in feedback and review
- assuming good intent while staying clear and direct
- focusing criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people
- sharing context and reproduction steps when reporting problems

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private information without permission
- deliberately disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer privately.
